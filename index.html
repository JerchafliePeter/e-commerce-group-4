Welcome to PeerME

About Us!

PeerMe is an online self-education platform that provides advantages to both the company and the users who utilize our unique take on gamified crowdsourced grading.

Learn More

Mission Statement

We will provide a platform for individuals to easily pursue online self- education whilst we motivate them to compete and learn from their peers via gamified crowdsourced grading.

Learn More

Business Philosophy

Encourage users to learn about a wide variety of topics. Strive to offer a platform that inspires users to educate themselves using the wealth of knowledge already available on the internet. Challenge users by establishing a competitive environment. The platform and the users both benefit from a simple solution to the grading aspect of online education.
