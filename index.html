Welcome to PeerME

About Us!

PeerMe is an online self-education platform that provides a unique take on gamified crowdsourced grading.

Learn More

Mission Statement

We will provide a platform for individuals to easily pursue online self-education whilst we motivate them to compete and learn from their peers via gamified crowdsourced grading.

Learn More

Business Philosophy

We encourage others to learn about a wide variety of topics. We srive to offer a platform that inspires users to educate themselves using the wealth of knowledge available at their fingertips. We challenge users by establishing a competitive environment. Our revolutionary platform provides users both with a simple and effective solution to the grading aspect of online education.
