When will my submission be reviewed?
Submission reviews are often completed in about 24 hours, something that PeerMe strives for! Due to the high volume of submissions and the time it may take your submission reviewer to give personalized feedback, the turnaround period for submission evaluations may be up to one week.
How do I cancel a submission?
If the option is available you can now cancel a submission after it has been made. Once a submission has been picked up to review you will not have the option to cancel it. If you would like to cancel your submission for a project you will first want to navigate to the project page. Once there you will see a link to Cancel Submission in the upper right hand corner of the page.
How does gamified crowdsourced grading work?
For each submission you make, we provide you with a rubric which will be used to grade it. Once you've made a submission, we will provide you with a random submission from one of your peers taking the same course and you will check it against the rubric. Afterwards, you will submit your review and any feedback you may have through our site. Your submission will be given a Meets Specifications grade if you've successfully completed the rubric requirements. If the reviewer determines that one or more items were not met, you will receive a Requires Changes grade. You'll need to make the necessary corrections, then submit again. You will only get one chance to re-submit. Once all submissions from a given course are reviewed, awards and achievements will be distributed accordingly. Users may check their standing on the Leaderboard page.
